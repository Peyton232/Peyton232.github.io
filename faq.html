Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

HandyAndy Handyman Service proudly serves the greater Houston area. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, please feel free to contact us for further information.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, HandyAndy Handyman Service is fully licensed and insured. We prioritize safety and professionalism, ensuring that our clients have peace of mind throughout the project.

How do I request a service?

To request a service, simply reach out to us via phone or email. Andy will be happy to discuss your needs, answer any questions you may have, and schedule an appointment at a time convenient for you.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept various forms of payment, including cash, credit cards, and electronic bank transfers. At the completion of the project, we will provide you with an invoice outlining the services rendered and the total cost.
